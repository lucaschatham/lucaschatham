A simple, focused habit tracking dashboard I built to help myself stay consistent with daily routines.

## Why I built it

Most habit trackers are bloated with features I don't need. I wanted something minimal: a grid showing what I did today, this week, and over time. No gamification, no social features, just data.

## What it does

- **Daily check-ins**: Mark habits as done each day
- **Streak tracking**: See current and longest streaks at a glance
- **Weekly and monthly views**: Spot patterns in consistency over time

## Tech stack

Built with Next.js, TypeScript, and Supabase. Deployed on Vercel at [habits.lucaschatham.com](https://habits.lucaschatham.com).
