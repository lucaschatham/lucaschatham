Daybreaker Health is a health technology company I'm building that focuses on personalized wellness and longevity protocols.

## What it does

The platform helps health practitioners deliver personalized protocols to their clients, covering everything from movement and nutrition to sleep and recovery.

## Key features

- **Protocol engine**: Practitioners design custom protocols using a structured template system
- **Client dashboard**: Clients see their active protocols, progress, and upcoming sessions
- **Data integration**: Connects with wearables and lab data to track outcomes

## Tech stack

Built with Next.js, TypeScript, Supabase, and deployed on Vercel. The architecture prioritizes simplicity and reliability over complexity.
