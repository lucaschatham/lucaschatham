I'm a big believer that good tools amplify good habits. Here are some of the things I reach for every day.

- **VS Code** with a minimal setup (just a handful of extensions)
- **Claude Code** for AI-assisted development
- **GitHub** for version control and collaboration
- **Vercel** for deployment

## Productivity

- **Todoist** for task management
- **Obsidian** for notes and knowledge management
- **Notion** for team collaboration

## Health

- **Whoop** for recovery and strain tracking
- **Oura Ring** for sleep data
- **Cronometer** for nutrition tracking

I'll keep this list updated as things change.
